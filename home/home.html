Meet Dr. Chad Coffeen

Dr. Chad Edward Smith was born and raised in the Walla Walla Valley. Attended Walla Walla Valley Academy before earning his prerequisites at Walla Walla Community College. In 2007, he began studying at Palmer College of Chiropractic West, in San Jose, California. During his time at Palmer West, he participated in extra learning opportunities such as the Palmer West Sports Counsel, who provided care in local sporting events, and regular tutoring for fellow students.

Dr. Smith began his internship at Stateline Chiropractic in the fall of 2010, and was hired on as a fellow provider in the spring of 2011. He has enjoyed his career thus far at the clinic. Stateline Chiropractic employees a great team, and he feels fortunate to provide care in the community where he grew up.

Dr. Smith and all his siblings live in the Walla Walla area. He and his wife, Kristen, enjoy being uncle and aunt to six, soon be seven, nieces and nephews. In his free time, he enjoys his lifelong hobbies of fishing and sports. He fishes regularly with his family, and continues to play basketball and softball in the community each year.
