The Problem: Sublaxations

Subluxations occur when one or more of your vertebrae shift out of optimal position, causing them to be too rigid (or not rigid enough), putting pressure on your spinal nerves, straining your muscles, and potentially damaging the soft tissue in surrounding areas. This can cause pain, decreased range of motion in multiple areas of the body, and an overall decrease in health and wellness.

Trauma to our bodies in the form of disease, physical accidents, and even normal life experiences, like pregnancy, moving to a new home, or starting a new job can all cause subluxations. When your body is healthy and functioning at 100%, you feel well, are pain free, and have a better quality of life. Removing subluxations will help you to experience a higher level of health and wellness.

The Experience: What Does the Pro Adjuster Feel Like?

While you sit in a special chair, one of our Doctors of Chiropractic moves a small, U-shaped instrument down your spine, touching each vertebra and allowing the Pro Adjuster to detect areas of misalignment. After that, the doctor holds the instrument on the problem vertebra and the machine uses a series of gentle taps to shift the vertebra back into optimal position. There are no sudden movements associated with this type of adjustment. After this, the doctor many use the Pro Adjuster on other joints, like your shoulder or jaw, using the same tapping method as before.
